# marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
**Primary Positioning Statement**: AnimeMetaFix is an AI-powered service that automatically detects, corrects, and reorders metadata for anime collections in Jellyfin and similar media servers, ensuring your collection is always accurate and organized.

**Alternative Positioning Statements**:
1. Transform your anime collection with AnimeMetaFix: the AI tool that perfects metadata for seamless viewing experiences.
2. Say goodbye to metadata chaos! AnimeMetaFix intelligently organizes your anime library for optimal enjoyment.
3. Enhance your self-hosted media server with AnimeMetaFix: the smart solution for flawless anime metadata management.

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
1. **Persona 1: Alex the Anime Enthusiast**
   - **Daily Job**: Manages a personal anime collection on Jellyfin, spends time curating and organizing content.
   - **Budget**: $15/month for tools that enhance media management.

2. **Persona 2: Jamie the Media Server Admin**
   - **Daily Job**: Oversees a community media server, ensuring all content is correctly categorized and accessible for users.
   - **Budget**: $50/month for tools that improve server functionality and user experience.

3. **Persona 3: Sam the Content Curator**
   - **Daily Job**: Works for a small streaming service, responsible for maintaining accurate metadata for a diverse library of anime titles.
   - **Budget**: $200/month for professional-grade tools that streamline metadata management.

## Channels
1. **Content Marketing (Blog & SEO)**
   - **CAC Estimate**: $20 per acquired customer.
   - Focus on creating valuable content around anime metadata management, Jellyfin tips, and AI tools for media servers.

2. **Social Media Advertising (Facebook & Instagram)**
   - **CAC Estimate**: $30 per acquired customer.
   - Target anime communities and media server users with engaging ads showcasing the benefits of AnimeMetaFix.

3. **Partnerships with Anime Communities & Forums**
   - **CAC Estimate**: $15 per acquired customer.
   - Collaborate with popular anime forums and communities to promote AnimeMetaFix through sponsored posts and affiliate marketing.
