# Business Model Canvas for AnimeMetaFix

## Customer Segments
- **Self-Hosted Media Server Users**: Individuals and small businesses using Jellyfin and similar platforms for anime collections.
- **Anime Enthusiasts**: Users who prioritize accurate metadata for better organization and discovery of content.
- **Media Server Administrators**: Professionals managing media libraries for organizations or communities, seeking efficiency in metadata management.

## Value Propositions
- **Automated Metadata Correction**: AI-driven service that detects and corrects metadata issues, saving users time and effort.
- **Enhanced User Experience**: Improved organization and discoverability of anime collections, leading to higher user satisfaction.
- **Cost-Effective Solution**: Affordable pricing compared to manual correction efforts or hiring external services, estimated at $10/month (≈ 350 THB).

## Channels
- **Direct Sales**: Website offering subscriptions and service packages.
- **GitHub Repository**: Open-source presence to attract developers and tech-savvy users.
- **Social Media & Forums**: Engagement through anime and tech communities on platforms like Reddit and Discord.

## Customer Relationships
- **Community Engagement**: Building a user community for feedback and feature requests through forums and social media.
- **Customer Support**: Offering responsive support via email and chat for troubleshooting and inquiries.
- **Freemium Model**: Providing a basic version for free to attract users, with premium features available for a subscription fee.

## Revenue Streams
- **Subscription Fees**: Monthly subscription model at $10/month (≈ 350 THB) for premium features.
- **One-Time Purchases**: Option for users to buy specific enhancements or features.
- **Donations**: Encouraging contributions from users who appreciate the open-source model.

## Key Resources
- **AI Algorithms**: Proprietary algorithms for metadata detection and correction.
- **Development Team**: Skilled engineers and data scientists for ongoing product development and support.
- **Community Contributions**: Leveraging open-source contributions for continuous improvement and feature expansion.

## Key Activities
- **Product Development**: Continuous improvement of AI algorithms and user interface based on user feedback.
- **Marketing & Outreach**: Promoting the service through social media, forums, and partnerships with anime communities.
- **Customer Support**: Providing assistance and gathering user feedback to enhance the product.

## Key Partners
- **Media Server Platforms**: Partnerships with Jellyfin and similar platforms for integration and promotion.
- **Anime Communities**: Collaborations with anime forums and social media groups for outreach and user acquisition.
- **Open Source Contributors**: Engaging with the open-source community for feature development and bug fixes.

## Cost Structure
- **Development Costs**: Salaries for developers and data scientists, estimated at $100,000/year (≈ 3,500,000 THB).
- **Marketing Expenses**: Budget for online advertising and community engagement, estimated at $20,000/year (≈ 700,000 THB).
- **Operational Costs**: Hosting, support infrastructure, and maintenance, estimated at $10,000/year (≈ 350,000 THB).
